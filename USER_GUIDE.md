# Nutri5 - HealthyChat User Guide

Welcome to Nutri5! Your personal AI nutritionist available 24/7.

## Getting Started

### 1. Create Your Account

1. Visit the Nutri5 website
2. Click "Get Started" button
3. Fill in your details:
   - Full Name
   - Email address
   - Secure password (minimum 6 characters)
4. Confirm your email address (check your inbox)
5. You're ready to chat!

### 2. First Time Login

1. Enter your email and password
2. You'll be directed to the chat interface
3. Start your nutrition consultation

## Chat Interface

### Text Chat

**How to use:**
1. Type your nutrition question in the input field
2. Ask anything about:
   - Dietary advice
   - Meal planning
   - Health goals
   - Food allergies and restrictions
   - Nutrition facts
3. Click the send button (arrow icon)
4. AI nutritionist responds with personalized advice

**Tips for better responses:**
- Be specific about your health goals
- Mention any dietary restrictions
- Share your current diet if relevant
- Ask follow-up questions for clarification

### Audio Chat

**How to use audio:**

1. **Enable Audio Mode**
   - Click "Audio On" button in the chat interface
   
2. **Record Your Question**
   - Click the microphone icon
   - Speak your question clearly
   - Click the stop button (red square)
   - AI transcribes and responds

3. **Listen to Responses**
   - Click "Hear" button on AI responses
   - AI reads the answer using text-to-speech
   - Perfect for hands-free consultation

4. **Type Response**
   - You can always type instead of speaking
   - Combine audio and text as needed

**Audio Tips:**
- Speak clearly for better transcription
- Use a quiet environment
- Allow microphone access when prompted
- Internet required for audio features

## Features

### Dark Mode

1. **Enable Dark Mode**
   - Click the moon icon in the top right
   - Perfect for comfortable reading at night
   - Automatically respects system preferences

2. **Disable Dark Mode**
   - Click the sun icon to return to light mode

### Preferences

Access your preferences from the chat interface:
- **Audio on/off** - Toggle audio features
- **Dark mode** - Switch between light and dark themes
- **Session history** - Your chat is saved automatically

### Privacy

- Your conversations are private and secure
- Only you can see your chat history
- Audio is transcribed immediately and not stored
- Health data is encrypted and protected

## Common Questions

### How does Nutri5 work?

Nutri5 uses advanced AI (GPT-4) trained on nutrition science to provide personalized advice. It considers:
- Your health goals
- Dietary preferences
- Medical conditions
- Available foods in your region
- Budget constraints

### Is this a substitute for a doctor?

No. Nutri5 provides nutrition guidance but cannot:
- Diagnose medical conditions
- Replace professional medical advice
- Prescribe medications
- Provide emergency medical care

**Always consult a healthcare provider for serious health concerns.**

### Can I use audio?

Yes! Nutri5 supports:
- Speech-to-text (you speak, AI transcribes)
- Text-to-speech (AI reads responses)
- Text input as alternative

You need a microphone and internet for audio features.

### Is my data private?

Yes. Your data is:
- Encrypted in transit and at rest
- Protected by Row Level Security
- Only accessible to you
- Never sold or shared with third parties

### What if I forgot my password?

1. Click "Sign in" on the login page
2. Look for password recovery option
3. Enter your email address
4. Follow the recovery instructions

### How long are conversations saved?

Your conversations are saved indefinitely until you delete them. You can:
- Review past conversations
- Continue previous consultations
- Reference advice at any time

### Can I use this offline?

No, Nutri5 requires an internet connection for:
- AI responses
- Audio transcription
- Text-to-speech

## Tips for Better Results

### Effective Nutrition Questions

**Good questions:**
- "I want to gain muscle. What should I eat daily?"
- "I have diabetes. What are good breakfast options?"
- "My budget is $50/week. What should I eat?"
- "I'm vegetarian. How do I get enough protein?"

**Avoid vague questions:**
- "What's a good diet?" (too broad)
- "What should I eat?" (no context)

4. **Follow up**

   - Clarify nutritional info

## Troubleshooting

### Audio Not Working

**Problem:** Microphone not responding
- Check browser permissions
- Allow microphone access
- Try refreshing the page
- Check internet connection

**Problem:** Can't hear AI response
- Check volume levels
- Try headphones
- Refresh and try again
- Use text instead

### Chat Issues

**Problem:** AI not responding
- Check internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Try a simpler question
- Check if you're logged in

**Problem:** Can't log in
- Check email and password
- Confirm email address
- Try password recovery
- Clear browser cache

### Performance

**Problem:** App running slow
- Clear browser cache
- Close unused tabs
- Restart browser
- Check internet speed
